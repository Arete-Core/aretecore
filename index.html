Our Name

The Meaning
Behind the Name

Arête — The Ridge

In mountaineering and climbing, an arête is a sharp ridge or corner where two rock faces meet. Climbers use arêtes as leverage points to gain height and move closer to their summit or objective.

Core — The Center

Core represents what sits at the center of our work — people, principles, and technology. Like processor cores, we understand how individual components work together to perform meaningful work at scale.

The Principle

Arête Core applies this same principle to technology — using systems and infrastructure as leverage to propel organizations higher.
